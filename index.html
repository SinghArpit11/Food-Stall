Idlly

It is one of the go to breakfast in the Southern-part of India.It will be full-filling as well as good for health. Beacuse in this very less amount of oil is used. It is eaten with Sambhar, Coconut-Chatney,Rasam.

Dosa

It is one of the go to breakfast in the Southern-part of India.It will be full-filling . It is eaten with Sambhar, Coconut-Chatney,Rasam.

Gol-Gappa

It is basically named variabliiy among different parts of India.Commnly in Western part it will be called Pani-Puri. In Northern part commonly Known as Puchka.In Central part it is known as Gol-Gappa. It will be having different fillings of khatta paani(sour-salty water),meetha(sweet-water),Teekha(spicy-water) with the filling of potato .

Raj-kachaur

It is the Famous food in Northern part of the country . Specially in Delhi. It is good to have at breakfast it is full-filling but in this a lot of oil is used . It is having the filling of potato and chana dal and served with curd , meetha chutney(sweet-sauce) and some namkeen.

Rasgulla

It is basically the desert . It is the dish of Eastern part of India.It is first made in Odisha . But loved by everyone . It is made up of Milk .
